Jazz Virk
Jazz has made a career in photography and has been in the film, television, digital media, and print industries for the past 22 years. He has worked on commercials, music videos, documentaries, and feature films in Canada, the USA, India, and the UK.



Some of his former clients are Apple, RBC, Sports Illustrated, BBC, CBC, Rogers, Bayer Aspirin, Pioneer, Unico, Alexanian, Shoppers Drug Mart, Echo TV, Cottage Life Television and MuchMusic.



Some of the artists Jazz has worked with are Nelly Furtado, Enrique Iglesias, Jazzy B, Sonu Nigam, Gunjan from Thievery Corporation, Jay Sean, Charlene and Slata Jon from Basement Jaxx.



Jazz studied film & photography at U of T and Sheridan College.  Jazz also teaches and has taught workshops at Ealing Studios UK, TMU, Dufferin Peel School Board and through

workshops at the  International Film Festival of South Asia.
